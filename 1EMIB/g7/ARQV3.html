Rollercoaster:

Author: Rodrigo Perri

The Kingda Ka is currently the tallest roller coaster in the world, reaching 139 meters high (the height of a building of 40 floors), and reaches a speed of up to 206 km/h. The ride lasts up to 56 seconds and the length is 950.4 meters. On holidays, a person can stand more than an hour in line to enter the attraction. Kingda Ka is located at Six Flags Great Adventure in Jackson, New Jersey, United States.
