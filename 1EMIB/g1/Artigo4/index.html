The worlds, also known as League of Legends World Championship, this year takes place the eleventh edition of the championship in Iceland, there we find the greatest players in the competitive scene in the world. The worlds had its first edition in 2011 in Sweden, whose winner was the British team Fnatic, the biggest winner of the tournament are the South Korean Skt, led by mid laner Faker, who are going in search of the fourth world title.
