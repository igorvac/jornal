COUNTER-STRIKE vs VALORANT: These two are fps style games. In this game we have two teams, attackers and defenders. The attackers' main objective is to plant a bomb in an area and not let the defenders disarm the bomb. Defenders, on the other hand, cannot let enemies plant the bomb, and if the bomb is planted, the objective is to defuse the bomb before it explodes. Valorant: Valorant is a fps game but with unique characters differential that have their own abilities. Counter-Strike: This game is a common fps but it was the most played free fps and what changed the history of fps, many championships and inspiration for other games. Differences: The main differences are the variety of what characters can do. In Counter-Strike you can use Molotovs and flashes whenever you want. In Valorant they are divided and each one has their abilities divided, and with that to work more as a team and not play alone.
